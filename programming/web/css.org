* 选取器
** 属性

| =[attr]=                                 |                                 |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| =[attr=value]=                           | 等于 =value=                    |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| =[attr~=value]=                          | （空格分隔）包含 =value=        |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| @@html:<code>[attr&#124;=value]</code>@@ | 等于 =value= 或以 =value-= 开头 |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| =[attr^=value]=                          | 以 =value= 开头                 |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| =[attr$=value]=                          | 以 =value= 结尾                 |
|------------------------------------------+---------------------------------|
| =[attr*=value]=                          | =value= 至少出现一次            |
